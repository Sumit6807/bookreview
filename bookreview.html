    5. The Alchemist

    "The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho is a philosophical novel about Santiago, a young shepherd who dreams of finding treasure in the Egyptian pyramids. His journey is both physical and spiritual, filled with encounters that teach him about following his dreams and listening to his heart. The story's simple yet profound message encourages readers to pursue their personal legends and recognize the interconnectedness of all things. With its poetic prose and uplifting themes, "The Alchemist" is a beloved and inspiring read.

    10. The Good Girl's Guide to Murder

    "A Good Girl's Guide to Murder" by Holly Jackson is a gripping YA mystery novel. It follows high school student Pippa Fitz-Amobi as she re-investigates a closed murder case for her senior project. Determined to uncover the truth behind the disappearance of Andie Bell and the subsequent suicide of her alleged killer, Sal Singh, Pippa discovers hidden secrets and shocking twists. The novel is praised for its engaging plot, strong character development, and suspenseful storytelling, making it a compelling read for fans of the genre.

    11. it starts with us

    "It Starts With Us" is a powerful and evocative tale that delves deep into the intricacies of relationships, family dynamics, and the impact of choices made in the past on the present. With rich character development and a compelling narrative, it explores themes of love, forgiveness, and the resilience of the human spirit. The storytelling is poignant, drawing readers into the lives of its characters and leaving a lasting impression. Overall, "It Starts With Us" is a thought-provoking read that resonates long after the final page.

    12. The song of ice and fire

    "The Song of Fire and Ice," famously known as "A Song of Ice and Fire," is an epic fantasy series that captivates with its intricate world-building, complex characters, and unpredictable plot twists. George R.R. Martin weaves a tapestry of political intrigue, power struggles, and supernatural elements against the backdrop of a vividly imagined medieval world. With its richly detailed narrative and morally ambiguous characters, the series challenges conventions of the genre and keeps readers eagerly turning pages. "A Song of Ice and Fire" is a masterpiece of modern fantasy literature, setting a high standard for the genre as a whole.
